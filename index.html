What mood are you in?

Clicking on a mood button gets you a game recommendation matching the mood you chose! If you don't like the suggestion then click the "get me a diffrent game" button and we will give you another suggestion from that mood button! Once you're satisfied with the results then click the tunes button and well give you a song suggestion to listen to while you game!
